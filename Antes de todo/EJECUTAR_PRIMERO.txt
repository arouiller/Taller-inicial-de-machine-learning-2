Descargar anaconda 
https://www.anaconda.com/download

Recrear entorno (basado en)
https://medium.com/swlh/setting-up-a-conda-environment-in-less-than-5-minutes-e64d8fc338e4
conda env create -f entorno_analisis_cervezas.yml

Quitar entorno
conda remove --name ENV_NAME (analisis_cervezas) --all

Activar entorno
conda activate analisis_cervezas

Lanzar Jupiter lab
jupiter lab
